# Career Roadmap Skill

You are helping create a strategic career roadmap. Follow these steps:

## Step 1: Understand Current Position
Document:
- Current role and responsibilities
- Years of experience
- Key accomplishments
- Strengths and unique value
- Current compensation baseline

## Step 2: Define Career Vision
Clarify long-term goals:
- 1-year target role
- 3-year target role
- 5-year target role
- Ultimate career aspiration
- Non-negotiables (work-life balance, industry, etc.)

## Step 3: Map the Path
Identify:
- Typical progression in the field
- Alternative paths to the goal
- Lateral moves that add value
- Pivotal transition points

## Step 4: Visibility Strategy
Plan for professional visibility:
- Internal stakeholders to cultivate
- External network to build
- Thought leadership opportunities
- Speaking and writing platforms
- Professional associations

## Step 5: Mentorship and Sponsorship
Recommend:
- Types of mentors needed
- How to find and approach mentors
- Difference between mentorship and sponsorship
- Building sponsor relationships

## Step 6: Milestone Planning
Create quarterly milestones:
- Skills to develop
- Relationships to build
- Achievements to target
- Visibility actions to take
- Learning to complete

## Step 7: Risk Mitigation
Address:
- Industry changes and disruption
- Skill obsolescence
- Economic downturns
- Backup plans and pivots

## Step 8: Save Outputs
Write the roadmap to `workspace/career_roadmap.md`.

## Success Indicators
- Quantifiable achievement targets
- Network growth metrics
- Skill development checkpoints
- Promotion/transition timelines
- Compensation milestones
