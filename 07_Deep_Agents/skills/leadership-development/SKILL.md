# Leadership Development Skill

You are helping develop leadership capabilities. Follow these steps:

## Step 1: Assess Leadership Readiness
Evaluate current:
- Leadership experience (formal and informal)
- Team management exposure
- Decision-making responsibilities
- Influence and impact scope

## Step 2: Identify Leadership Style
Explore leadership preferences:
- Directive vs. collaborative
- Detail-oriented vs. big-picture
- People-focused vs. results-focused
- Risk tolerance level

## Step 3: Core Competency Development
Build skills in:
- **Communication**: Clear, inspiring, adaptable messaging
- **Delegation**: Effective task assignment and follow-up
- **Feedback**: Constructive, timely, actionable
- **Conflict Resolution**: Mediation and problem-solving
- **Strategic Thinking**: Big-picture planning

## Step 4: Emotional Intelligence
Develop:
- Self-awareness (understanding own triggers and biases)
- Self-regulation (managing reactions)
- Empathy (understanding others' perspectives)
- Social skills (building rapport and trust)
- Motivation (inspiring self and others)

## Step 5: Leadership Opportunities
Identify ways to practice:
- Volunteer for project leadership
- Mentor junior colleagues
- Lead cross-functional initiatives
- Take on stretch assignments
- Join professional leadership programs

## Step 6: Build Your Leadership Brand
Define:
- Your leadership philosophy
- Core values you lead by
- How you want to be known
- Stories that illustrate your leadership

## Step 7: Create Development Plan
Structure a 90-day plan with:
- Weekly focus areas
- Practice opportunities
- Feedback mechanisms
- Reflection points

## Step 8: Save Outputs
Write the development plan to `workspace/leadership_development.md`.

## Leadership Books to Consider
- "The First 90 Days" by Michael Watkins
- "Leaders Eat Last" by Simon Sinek
- "Radical Candor" by Kim Scott
- "The Making of a Manager" by Julie Zhuo
