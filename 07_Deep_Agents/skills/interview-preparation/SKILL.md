# Interview Preparation Skill

You are helping prepare for a job interview. Follow these steps:

## Step 1: Understand the Opportunity
Gather information about:
- Company name and industry
- Role and responsibilities
- Interview format (phone, video, panel, technical)
- Interview stage (screening, technical, final)

## Step 2: Research Company
Investigate and document:
- Company mission and values
- Recent news and developments
- Products/services offered
- Company culture indicators
- Key competitors

## Step 3: Prepare Common Questions
Generate answers for:
- "Tell me about yourself" (2-minute elevator pitch)
- "Why this company?" (company-specific response)
- "Why this role?" (role-specific motivation)
- "Strengths and weaknesses" (honest, growth-oriented)
- "Where do you see yourself in 5 years?"

## Step 4: Behavioral Questions (STAR Method)
Prepare 5-7 STAR stories covering:
- Leadership/initiative
- Conflict resolution
- Problem-solving
- Teamwork/collaboration
- Failure and learning
- Achievement under pressure

## Step 5: Technical/Role-Specific Prep
Based on the role, prepare:
- Technical knowledge demonstrations
- Case study approaches
- Portfolio walkthrough talking points
- Relevant project deep-dives

## Step 6: Questions to Ask Interviewer
Prepare 5-10 thoughtful questions about:
- Team structure and dynamics
- Success metrics for the role
- Growth opportunities
- Company challenges and goals
- Day-to-day responsibilities

## Step 7: Save Outputs
Write the interview prep guide to `workspace/interview_prep_guide.md`.

## Interview Day Tips
- Arrive 10-15 minutes early (or test tech for virtual)
- Bring copies of resume and notepad
- Practice confident body language
- Listen actively and ask clarifying questions
- Send thank-you email within 24 hours
