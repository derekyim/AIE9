# LinkedIn Optimization Skill

You are helping optimize a LinkedIn profile. Follow these steps:

## Step 1: Understand Goals
Determine the user's objectives:
- Job searching (active or passive)
- Networking and thought leadership
- Business development
- Industry visibility
- Career transition

## Step 2: Profile Audit
Review current profile elements:
- Headline (120 characters max)
- About section (2,600 characters max)
- Experience descriptions
- Skills and endorsements
- Recommendations
- Profile photo and banner

## Step 3: Headline Optimization
Create a compelling headline that:
- Goes beyond job title
- Includes target keywords
- Communicates value proposition
- Appeals to target audience

Example formats:
- "Role | Specialty | Value Proposition"
- "Helping [audience] achieve [outcome] through [method]"

## Step 4: About Section
Write an engaging summary that:
- Opens with a hook (first 3 lines visible)
- Tells a professional story
- Highlights key achievements
- Includes relevant keywords
- Ends with a call to action

## Step 5: Experience Optimization
For each role:
- Use impactful headlines
- Lead with achievements, not duties
- Include metrics and results
- Add relevant media (presentations, links)

## Step 6: Skills and Recommendations
Recommend:
- Top 50 skills (ordered by relevance)
- 3-5 people to request recommendations from
- Template for requesting recommendations

## Step 7: Engagement Strategy
Suggest a content strategy:
- Posting frequency
- Content types (articles, posts, comments)
- Hashtags and keywords
- Networking activities

## Step 8: Save Outputs
Write optimized content to `workspace/linkedin_profile.md`.

## Keywords to Include
- Industry-specific terms
- Job title variations
- Technical skills
- Soft skills
- Certifications
