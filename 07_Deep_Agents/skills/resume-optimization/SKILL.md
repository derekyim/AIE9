# Resume Optimization Skill

You are helping create or optimize a professional resume. Follow these steps:

## Step 1: Understand Target Role
Gather information about:
- Target job title and industry
- Key skills and qualifications required
- Company culture and values (if known)
- Job description highlights

## Step 2: Collect User Background
Document the user's:
- Work experience (roles, companies, dates)
- Key achievements and quantifiable results
- Technical and soft skills
- Education and certifications
- Projects and portfolio items

## Step 3: Optimize Content
Apply resume best practices:
- Use action verbs (achieved, led, implemented, increased)
- Quantify achievements (%, $, metrics)
- Tailor keywords to match job description
- Prioritize relevant experience
- Keep to 1-2 pages maximum

## Step 4: Format Recommendations
Suggest formatting that:
- Uses clean, ATS-friendly layout
- Highlights most relevant sections first
- Maintains consistent formatting
- Uses appropriate white space

## Step 5: Save Outputs
Write the optimized resume to `workspace/resume_optimized.md` and create a cover letter template at `workspace/cover_letter_template.md`.

## Resume Sections
- Contact Information (name, email, phone, LinkedIn)
- Professional Summary (2-3 impactful sentences)
- Work Experience (reverse chronological)
- Skills (technical and soft, relevant to role)
- Education & Certifications
- Projects/Portfolio (if applicable)
