# Health Habits Skill

You are helping build foundational health habits. Follow these steps:

## Step 1: Assess Current Health Habits
Review current state:
- Nutrition (diet quality, hydration)
- Movement (exercise frequency, daily activity)
- Sleep (duration, quality)
- Stress management
- Preventive care (checkups, screenings)
- Substance use (alcohol, tobacco, etc.)

## Step 2: Identify Priority Areas
Rate each area (1-10):
- Nutrition: ___
- Hydration: ___
- Movement: ___
- Sleep: ___
- Stress: ___
- Social connection: ___

Focus on lowest scores first.

## Step 3: Foundational Health Pillars
Address basics:

### Nutrition Foundations
- Eat mostly whole foods
- Include protein at each meal
- Plenty of vegetables and fruits
- Minimize ultra-processed foods
- Moderate portions
- Mindful eating

### Hydration
- Aim for 64+ oz water daily
- Start day with water
- Hydrate before caffeine
- Limit sugary drinks
- Adjust for activity level

### Movement
- Aim for 150 min moderate activity/week
- Include strength training 2x/week
- Daily walking (7,000+ steps)
- Reduce prolonged sitting
- Stretch and mobilize

### Sleep
- 7-9 hours nightly
- Consistent schedule
- Dark, cool environment
- Wind-down routine

## Step 4: Create Keystone Habits
Identify one habit that triggers others:
- Morning walk → more energy → better food choices
- Meal prep → healthier eating → better energy
- Evening routine → better sleep → more willpower

## Step 5: Design Implementation
For each priority habit:
- Specific behavior
- When and where
- How to start small
- How to track
- Reward/celebration

## Step 6: Address Obstacles
Plan for:
- Time constraints
- Travel and disruption
- Social situations
- Low motivation days
- Setbacks and recovery

## Step 7: Preventive Health
Ensure:
- Annual physical exam
- Age-appropriate screenings
- Dental checkups
- Vision checks
- Vaccinations current

## Step 8: Create Weekly Health Template
Design ideal week:
- Workout schedule
- Meal prep time
- Sleep and wake times
- Stress management practices
- Social connection time

## Step 9: Track Progress
Monitor:
- Habit completion
- Energy levels
- Mood
- Physical symptoms
- Weight/measurements (if relevant)

## Step 10: Save Outputs
Write plan to `workspace/health_habits_plan.md`.

## Health Habit Tips
- One habit at a time
- Attach to existing routines
- Environment design matters
- Progress over perfection
- Self-compassion for setbacks
