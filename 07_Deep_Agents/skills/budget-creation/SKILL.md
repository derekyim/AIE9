# Budget Creation Skill

You are helping create a personal budget. Follow these steps:

## Step 1: Gather Income Information
Document all income sources:
- Primary salary/wages (after tax)
- Side income or freelance
- Investment income
- Rental income
- Other regular income

## Step 2: Track Current Expenses
Categorize spending for the past 3 months:

### Fixed Expenses
- Housing (rent/mortgage)
- Utilities (electric, gas, water, internet)
- Insurance (health, auto, life)
- Loan payments
- Subscriptions

### Variable Expenses
- Groceries
- Transportation (gas, transit, rideshare)
- Dining out
- Entertainment
- Shopping
- Personal care

### Irregular Expenses
- Annual subscriptions
- Car maintenance
- Medical expenses
- Gifts and holidays
- Home repairs

## Step 3: Choose a Budgeting Method
Recommend based on personality:
- **50/30/20**: 50% needs, 30% wants, 20% savings
- **Zero-based**: Every dollar has a job
- **Envelope system**: Cash categories
- **Pay yourself first**: Savings first, then spend

## Step 4: Create Budget Categories
Design a monthly budget with:
- Income total
- Fixed expense allocations
- Variable expense limits
- Savings/investment targets
- Buffer for unexpected expenses

## Step 5: Identify Optimization Opportunities
Find areas to reduce:
- Subscriptions to cancel
- Negotiable bills (insurance, internet)
- Spending leaks
- Lifestyle inflation

## Step 6: Set Up Tracking System
Recommend tools:
- Spreadsheet template
- Budgeting apps (YNAB, Mint, etc.)
- Weekly check-in routine
- Monthly review process

## Step 7: Create Action Items
List specific steps:
- Bills to automate
- Accounts to open
- Subscriptions to cancel
- Spending alerts to set

## Step 8: Save Outputs
Write budget to `workspace/personal_budget.md`.

## Budget Review Schedule
- Weekly: Quick expense check
- Monthly: Full budget review
- Quarterly: Category adjustments
- Annually: Major goal reassessment
