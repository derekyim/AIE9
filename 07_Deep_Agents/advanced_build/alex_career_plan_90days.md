# Alex's 90-Day Career Advancement & Promotion Plan

**Goal**: Position yourself for promotion through strategic skill development, networking, and professional visibility  
**Focus**: Performance improvement, leadership development, professional branding, and measurable career growth

## 90-Day Promotion Strategy Overview

This plan systematically addresses the key areas that influence promotion decisions: performance, skills, relationships, and visibility. Each month builds on the previous to create cumulative career momentum.

## Month 1: Assess and Align (Days 1-30)

### Week 1: Identify Promotion Requirements (Days 1-7)
**Objective**: Create clear roadmap for promotion eligibility

**Daily Actions:**
- **Day 1-2**: Research specific role requirements for desired promotion
  - Review job descriptions for target position
  - Identify required skills, experiences, and performance metrics
  - Document reporting structure and key responsibilities
- **Day 3-4**: Analyze promotion criteria used by your organization
  - Review company promotion guidelines
  - Research recent promotions in your department
  - Understand decision-making process and timeline
- **Day 5-7**: Create comprehensive requirements list
  - Technical skills needed
  - Leadership competencies required
  - Performance standards expected
  - Experience gaps to address

**Week 1 Deliverable**: Complete promotion requirements document

### Week 2: Skill Gap Assessment (Days 8-14)
**Objective**: Understand current capabilities vs. promotion requirements

**Daily Actions:**
- **Day 8-10**: Conduct honest self-assessment
  - Rate current skills against requirements (1-10 scale)
  - Identify top 3 skill gaps to address
  - Assess leadership experience and opportunities
- **Day 11-12**: Gather external perspectives
  - Request feedback from trusted colleagues
  - Review recent performance evaluations
  - Consider 360-degree feedback if available
- **Day 13-14**: Prioritize development areas
  - Rank skill gaps by importance and feasibility
  - Identify quick wins vs. long-term development needs
  - Consider which gaps are promotion blockers

**Week 2 Deliverable**: Prioritized skill development plan

### Week 3: Set SMART Goals (Days 15-21)
**Objective**: Create specific, measurable objectives for 90-day period

**Daily Actions:**
- **Day 15-16**: Establish 90-day career goals
  - 3-5 specific promotion-focused objectives
  - Each goal includes timeline and success metrics
  - Mix of skill development, performance, and visibility goals
- **Day 17-18**: Break goals into monthly and weekly targets
  - Month 1: Assessment and foundation building
  - Month 2: Skill development and networking
  - Month 3: Performance demonstration and visibility
- **Day 19-21**: Create accountability system
  - Schedule regular self-check-ins
  - Identify mentor or colleague for progress discussions
  - Set up tracking method for goal progress

**Week 3 Deliverable**: SMART goals document with tracking system

### Week 4: Create Development Schedule (Days 22-30)
**Objective**: Establish structured approach for remaining 60 days

**Daily Actions:**
- **Day 22-24**: Design weekly development routine
  - Allocate time for skill building (5-7 hours/week)
  - Schedule networking activities (2-3 hours/week)
  - Plan visibility and performance activities
- **Day 25-27**: Research learning resources
  - Identify online courses for skill gaps
  - Find books, podcasts, and industry resources
  - Explore internal training opportunities
- **Day 28-30**: Establish baseline metrics
  - Document current performance indicators
  - Set up systems to track progress
  - Plan Month 1 review and Month 2 adjustments

**Week 4 Deliverable**: Structured 12-week development calendar

## Month 2: Skill Development and Networking (Days 31-60)

### Week 5: Engage with Mentors (Days 31-37)
**Objective**: Build support network for career advancement

**Daily Actions:**
- **Day 31-33**: Identify potential mentors within organization
  - List leaders in target role or department
  - Consider cross-functional mentors with promotion experience
  - Research backgrounds and identify connection points
- **Day 34-35**: Craft mentor outreach strategy
  - Prepare elevator pitch about career goals
  - Draft request messages focusing on learning vs. asking for favors
  - Plan specific questions and areas where you want guidance
- **Day 36-37**: Initiate mentor connections
  - Reach out to 2-3 potential mentors
  - Request brief coffee/virtual meetings
  - Follow up professionally and promptly

**Week 5 Target**: Connect with at least 2 potential mentors

### Week 6: Schedule Feedback Sessions (Days 38-44)
**Objective**: Establish regular performance dialogue with supervisor

**Daily Actions:**
- **Day 38-40**: Prepare for supervisor conversation
  - Draft agenda focusing on promotion readiness
  - Prepare questions about performance expectations
  - Document current achievements and contributions
- **Day 41-42**: Schedule bi-weekly check-ins with supervisor
  - Request regular feedback on promotion progress
  - Ask for specific areas to focus on
  - Understand supervisor's perspective on your readiness
- **Day 43-44**: Implement feedback system
  - Set up regular progress reviews
  - Create system for documenting feedback
  - Plan how to address any concerns raised

**Week 6 Target**: Establish bi-weekly supervisor feedback meetings

### Week 7: Professional Networking (Days 45-51)
**Objective**: Expand professional network and industry visibility

**Daily Actions:**
- **Day 45-47**: Research upcoming networking opportunities
  - Industry events, conferences, webinars
  - Professional association meetings
  - Internal company networking events
- **Day 48-49**: Attend at least one networking event
  - Prepare elevator pitch and conversation starters
  - Set goal to meet 3-5 new professional contacts
  - Practice active listening and authentic engagement
- **Day 50-51**: Follow up with new connections
  - Send personalized LinkedIn connection requests
  - Share relevant articles or resources with new contacts
  - Schedule follow-up conversations with most promising connections

**Week 7 Target**: Make 3 new professional connections and follow up

### Week 8: Personal Branding Development (Days 52-60)
**Objective**: Enhance professional presence and visibility

**Daily Actions:**
- **Day 52-54**: Audit current professional brand
  - Review LinkedIn profile for promotion alignment
  - Assess email signature, bio, and other professional materials
  - Identify gaps between current brand and target role
- **Day 55-57**: Update professional materials
  - Craft compelling personal branding statement
  - Update LinkedIn with recent achievements and skills
  - Ensure all materials reflect promotion aspirations
- **Day 58-60**: Increase professional visibility
  - Share industry insights on LinkedIn (aim for 5-10 endorsements)
  - Comment thoughtfully on leader posts in your network
  - Consider writing article or post about your expertise area

**Week 8 Target**: Achieve 5-10 LinkedIn endorsements/recommendations

## Month 3: Performance and Visibility (Days 61-90)

### Week 9: Progress Review and Adjustment (Days 61-67)
**Objective**: Assess progress and optimize final month strategy

**Daily Actions:**
- **Day 61-63**: Conduct comprehensive progress review
  - Evaluate goal achievement to date
  - Assess skill development progress
  - Review feedback from mentors and supervisor
- **Day 64-66**: Adjust strategy based on findings
  - Identify areas needing more focus
  - Pivot tactics that aren't working effectively
  - Double down on successful approaches
- **Day 67**: Plan final month priorities
  - Determine most important activities for remaining weeks
  - Set specific targets for final push toward promotion readiness

**Week 9 Target**: Optimized strategy for final month

### Week 10: Leadership Development (Days 68-74)
**Objective**: Demonstrate leadership capabilities required for promotion

**Daily Actions:**
- **Day 68-70**: Enroll in leadership training
  - Complete at least one online leadership course
  - Focus on areas most relevant to target role
  - Apply learnings immediately in current role
- **Day 71-73**: Seek leadership opportunities
  - Volunteer for project leadership role
  - Offer to mentor junior team member
  - Lead team meeting or presentation
- **Day 74**: Document leadership experiences
  - Create portfolio of leadership examples
  - Prepare stories for future promotion discussions

**Week 10 Target**: Complete one leadership course and demonstrate leadership

### Week 11: Continue Skill Development (Days 75-81)
**Objective**: Address remaining skill gaps with focused learning

**Daily Actions:**
- **Day 75-77**: Complete targeted skill courses
  - Finish 2 online courses related to identified gaps
  - Focus on skills most critical for promotion
  - Obtain certificates or credentials where possible
- **Day 78-80**: Apply new skills in current role
  - Find opportunities to practice new capabilities
  - Volunteer for projects that use new skills
  - Share knowledge with team members
- **Day 81**: Update skill portfolio
  - Document all new capabilities developed
  - Update resume and LinkedIn with new skills

**Week 11 Target**: Complete 2 skill-building courses with practical application

### Week 12: Final Review and Promotion Positioning (Days 82-90)
**Objective**: Prepare for promotion discussion and demonstrate readiness

**Daily Actions:**
- **Day 82-84**: Create promotion readiness portfolio
  - Document all achievements from 90-day period
  - Compile feedback from mentors and supervisor
  - Prepare specific examples of promotion-ready behaviors
- **Day 85-87**: Schedule promotion discussion with supervisor
  - Request formal meeting to discuss promotion timeline
  - Present portfolio of development and achievements
  - Ask for specific next steps and timeline
- **Day 88-90**: Plan continued development
  - Set goals for next 90-day period if promotion not immediate
  - Maintain momentum in skill development and networking
  - Continue building on foundation established

**Week 12 Target**: Complete promotion discussion with clear next steps

## Performance Improvement Strategies

### Daily Performance Habits
- Start each day with clear priorities aligned to promotion goals
- Document achievements and impact metrics
- Seek feedback on important projects before completion
- Volunteer for high-visibility assignments when possible

### Weekly Performance Reviews
- Assess progress toward promotion-relevant objectives
- Identify opportunities to exceed expectations
- Review and adjust workload to focus on highest-impact activities
- Prepare examples of leadership and initiative for discussions

### Monthly Performance Evaluation
- Compile achievements and impact metrics
- Gather feedback from colleagues and stakeholders
- Assess reputation and visibility within organization
- Adjust performance strategy based on promotion timeline

## Networking and Visibility Action Plan

### Internal Networking
- **Week 1-4**: Map key influencers in target department
- **Week 5-8**: Build relationships with 2-3 internal mentors
- **Week 9-12**: Expand network to include peers in target role

### External Networking
- **Month 1**: Research industry events and professional associations
- **Month 2**: Attend 1-2 networking events and follow up with connections
- **Month 3**: Maintain new relationships and look for collaboration opportunities

### Professional Visibility
- **Monthly**: Share expertise through LinkedIn posts or internal presentations
- **Bi-weekly**: Comment thoughtfully on industry discussions
- **Weekly**: Contribute valuable insights in team meetings and projects

## Leadership Development Track

### Month 1: Foundation
- Assess current leadership style and capabilities
- Identify leadership competencies required for target role
- Begin leadership reading and online learning

### Month 2: Practice
- Volunteer for project leadership opportunities
- Practice leadership skills in current role
- Seek feedback on leadership potential from mentors

### Month 3: Demonstration
- Lead high-visibility project or initiative
- Mentor junior colleague or intern
- Present leadership examples in promotion discussions

## Progress Tracking Templates

### Weekly Progress Check
1. Goals worked on this week and progress made
2. New skills or knowledge acquired
3. Networking activities completed
4. Leadership opportunities pursued
5. Feedback received from others
6. Challenges encountered and how addressed
7. Priorities for upcoming week

### Monthly Career Assessment
1. Overall progress toward promotion readiness (%)
2. Skill development achievements this month
3. Relationship building and networking progress
4. Performance improvements and recognition received
5. Leadership growth and opportunities taken
6. Visibility and branding improvements
7. Areas needing more focus in coming month

### 90-Day Promotion Readiness Review
1. All original goals achieved or progressed
2. Skill gaps addressed and new capabilities demonstrated
3. Strong mentor/supporter network established
4. Consistent high performance with documented impact
5. Leadership experience and potential clearly shown
6. Professional brand aligned with target role
7. Supervisor and key stakeholders aware of promotion interest
8. Clear next steps and timeline established for promotion

*Remember: Promotion is earned through consistent demonstration of readiness for the next level. Focus on adding value, building relationships, and showing leadership potential. This plan gives you a systematic approach to position yourself as the obvious choice when promotion opportunities arise.*
