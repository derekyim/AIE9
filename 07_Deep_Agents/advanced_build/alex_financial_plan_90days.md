# Alex's 90-Day Financial Improvement Plan

**Goal**: Improve finances and save money over 90 days through structured budgeting, expense reduction, and sustainable financial habits.

## Financial Goals Overview

### 90-Day Targets
- **Primary Savings Goal**: Save $1,200 ($400/month)
- **Expense Reduction**: Cut discretionary spending by 15%
- **Emergency Fund**: Build $500 emergency fund
- **Budget Adherence**: Stay within budget 90% of weeks

## Phase 1: Foundation (Days 1-30)

### Week 1: Financial Assessment & Goal Setting (Days 1-7)
**Daily Actions:**
- Day 1-3: Set specific savings target ($1,200 for 90 days)
- Day 4-6: Create comprehensive budget template
  - Document all income sources
  - Categorize expenses: Fixed (rent, utilities), Variable (groceries), Discretionary (entertainment)
  - Allocate specific amounts to each category
- Day 7: Review and adjust initial budget

**Weekly Goal**: Save $100, identify $50 in expense cuts

### Week 2: Implementation & Tracking (Days 8-14)
**Daily Actions:**
- Day 8-10: Set up tracking system (app or spreadsheet)
- Day 11-14: Implement money-saving techniques:
  - Start using coupons for groceries
  - Begin meal planning to reduce food waste
  - Download cashback apps
- Track every expense for the week

**Weekly Goal**: Save $100, implement meal planning

### Week 3: Optimization (Days 15-21)
**Daily Actions:**
- Day 15-17: Review first two weeks' spending patterns
- Day 18-21: Start building emergency fund
  - Open separate savings account if needed
  - Set up automatic transfer of $15/week
- Continue expense tracking

**Weekly Goal**: Save $100, start emergency fund

### Week 4: Habit Formation (Days 22-30)
**Daily Actions:**
- Day 22-24: Cancel unused subscriptions
- Day 25-27: Review and adjust budget based on first month data
- Day 28-30: Plan for Month 2 improvements

**Monthly Review**: Assess progress toward $400 savings goal and 10% expense reduction

## Phase 2: Optimization (Days 31-60)

### Week 5-6: Advanced Strategies
**Daily Actions:**
- Implement the 24-hour rule for non-essential purchases
- Research and switch to better deals (phone, insurance, utilities)
- Start using public transportation or carpooling more
- Continue automated emergency fund contributions ($15/week)

**Bi-weekly Goals**: Save $200, reduce monthly expenses by 12%

### Week 7-8: Mid-Point Assessment
**Daily Actions:**
- Conduct comprehensive financial review
- Adjust savings and spending targets based on progress
- Explore additional income opportunities if needed
- Increase emergency fund contribution to $20/week

**Bi-weekly Goals**: Save $200, build emergency fund to $250

## Phase 3: Mastery (Days 61-90)

### Week 9-10: Advanced Optimization
**Daily Actions:**
- Review all recurring expenses for additional cuts
- Implement advanced meal prep to reduce food costs
- Use price comparison apps for all purchases
- Continue building emergency fund

**Bi-weekly Goals**: Save $200, achieve 15% expense reduction

### Week 11-12: Sustainability & Future Planning
**Daily Actions:**
- Create systems for maintaining financial habits beyond 90 days
- Plan major purchases within budget framework
- Set financial goals for next quarter
- Complete emergency fund target ($500)

**Final Goals**: Achieve $1,200 total savings, complete $500 emergency fund

## Practical Money-Saving Strategies

### Immediate Actions
- Cancel unused subscriptions and memberships
- Use library instead of buying books/movies
- Cook meals at home instead of ordering out
- Use generic brands for household items
- Take advantage of free entertainment options

### Transportation Savings
- Use public transportation or bike when possible
- Carpool or rideshare for longer trips
- Combine errands into single trips
- Walk for short distances

### Food & Grocery Savings
- Plan meals weekly and stick to shopping list
- Use coupons and cashback apps
- Buy seasonal produce
- Prepare coffee/lunch at home
- Shop sales and stock up on non-perishables

### Utility & Home Savings
- Unplug electronics when not in use
- Use programmable thermostat
- Switch to LED bulbs
- Take shorter showers
- Air-dry clothes when possible
